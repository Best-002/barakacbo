ACTIVITIES

Sensitisation

Empowering Our Community Baraka CBO tackles critical issues like gender-based violence, STIs, HIV/AIDS, and family planning through educational workshops. We aim to create a healthier, more informed community.

Youth empowerment.

Baraka CBO empowers youth through skills training, leadership development, and entrepreneurship support. This equips them to lead, solve problems, and drive positive change in our community.

Environment Conservation

Ideally, this is ensuring that the surrounding which we live in is safe and healthy; It starts with us as individuals then to the outside world. The organisation will organise compaigns and road shows to bring to the attention to the public the importance of keeping our environment safe.

Mental wellness campaigns

We’re committed to supporting mental well-being among our youth. Our initiatives include workshops, stigma reduction, holistic approaches, and collaboration with local communities around. Let’s make a positive impact together

Community Empowerment

The youth will be mobilised to participate in such activities which will enable them to interact thereby, building social capital; In so doing, they will be empowered to know their rights and therefore, improving their esteem.
